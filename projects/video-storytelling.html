Overview

For this project, we created a bike advertisement video using Adobe Premiere Pro and Adobe Audition. The purpose of this project was to improve my video editing and audio production skills, focusing on how visuals and sound work together in advertising.

The goal was to create a short, engaging ad that communicates energy, motion, and excitement associated with bikes.

Process

I started by editing the video footage in Premiere Pro, focusing on pacing, transitions, and visual flow. I worked on cutting clips to match the rhythm and energy of the ad.

After completing the visual edit, I moved the audio into Audition to clean up sound, adjust levels, and enhance clarity. I edited background music and sound effects to match the motion and timing of the video.

Once the audio was finalized, I brought it back into Premiere Pro and made final adjustments to ensure the sound and visuals were well synced.

Final Product

Reflection

This project helped me understand the importance of audio in video advertising. I learned that even simple sound edits can make a video feel more professional and impactful.

One challenge was balancing music, sound effects, and visuals without overwhelming the viewer. This helped me improve my attention to detail and pacing.

Overall, this project improved my confidence using Premiere Pro and Audition and helped me build a stronger foundation in video editing and audio production for future advertising and media projects.
